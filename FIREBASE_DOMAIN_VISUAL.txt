THE PROBLEM:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

    Your App (localhost:5000)
           ↓
    Tries to use Google Sign-In
           ↓
    Firebase checks: "Is localhost:5000 authorized?"
           ↓
    Firebase says: "❌ NO! Not authorized!"
           ↓
    Error: "This domain is not authorized for OAuth operations"


THE SOLUTION:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

    Go to Firebase Console
           ↓
    Add localhost:5000 to authorized domains
           ↓
    Firebase checks: "Is localhost:5000 authorized?"
           ↓
    Firebase says: "✅ YES! It's authorized!"
           ↓
    Google Sign-In works! 🎉


STEP-BY-STEP FLOW:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

    1. Open Browser
       ↓
    2. Go to Firebase Console
       https://console.firebase.google.com/
       ↓
    3. Select Project
       unvibe-54ae1
       ↓
    4. Click Authentication
       (Left sidebar)
       ↓
    5. Click Settings
       (⚙️ icon at top)
       ↓
    6. Scroll to Authorized Domains
       ↓
    7. Click "Add domain"
       ↓
    8. Type: localhost:5000
       ↓
    9. Click "Add"
       ↓
    10. Wait 5-10 seconds
        (Firebase syncs)
       ↓
    11. Clear Browser Cache
        (Cmd+Shift+Delete on Mac)
       ↓
    12. Hard Refresh
        (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)
       ↓
    13. Test Google Sign-In
        http://localhost:5000/login
       ↓
    14. ✅ SUCCESS!
