## Troubleshooting

### Issue: "Firebase not initialized"
**Solution:**
1. Press Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac) to clear cache
2. Press Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) to hard refresh
3. Try again

### Issue: "Only @newhorizonindia.edu emails are allowed"
**Solution:**
- Make sure your email ends with `@newhorizonindia.edu`
- Check for typos in the email

### Issue: "Invalid credentials"
**Solution:**
- Make sure you entered the correct password
- Check if your account exists
- Try signing up if you don't have an account

### Issue: Redirects to login page instead of dashboard
**Solution:**
1. Clear browser cache (Cmd+Shift+Delete)
2. Hard refresh (Cmd+Shift+R)
3. Try again
